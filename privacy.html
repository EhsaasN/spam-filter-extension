Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Spam Filter Extension ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension that integrates with your Gmail account.

Information We Access

Our extension requests access to your Gmail account with the following permissions:

  • gmail.readonly: To read your email messages for spam classification purposes. We do NOT store or share your emails.
  • gmail.modify: To move identified spam messages to the Spam folder or apply labels accordingly.

How We Use Your Data

We only use your Gmail data to provide the spam filtering functionality. Your emails are processed solely on your device or securely via authorized Google APIs, and no email content is stored on our servers.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data. Since we do not store email content, the risk of data leakage is minimized.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties except where required by law or Google API usage policies.
